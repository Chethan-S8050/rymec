ABOUT DEPARTMENT

An Independent Department for Computer Science and Engineering was established in the year 1987. The Department provides Undergraduate with the intake 180, Post graduate (M Tech) with the intake 18 and has VTU Recognized Research Center. The Department of Computer Science is the first NBA accredited course in Gulbarga region. The department aims at implanting the spirit of ingenuity, innovativeness and technical competency in the students through regular guidance. Department infrastructure includes well-furnished class rooms with wi-fi to support focused learning, state of art labs to impart knowledge in the most Hi-Tech environment and well stocked department library.


Vision

To build Professionally Excellent, Knowledgeable, Globally Competitive, Social Responsible Computer Engineers and Entrepreneurs”.



Mission

Md1 To provide quality education in computer science and engineering
Md2 To establish a continuous Industry Institute Interaction, Participation, collaboration to contribute job oriented skilled IT Engineers
Md3 To build human values, social values, entrepreneur skills and professional ethics among the IT technocrats
Md4 To focus on innovation and development of technologies by engaging in machine intelligence research areas

PEOs

PEO1 Engage in Design, Developing, testing and maintaining the software in the field of Computer Science & Engineering
PEO2 Solve real time problems of social relevance by applying the knowledge of Computer Engineering and/or pursue higher education and/or Entrepreneurship
PEO3 Engage in lifelong learning, career enhancement, research and adapt to changing potential needs of industry and society.

PSOs

PSO1 Graduates will be capable of modelling, designing, implementing and verifying a computer Network system to meet specified requirements.
PSO2 Graduates will be capable of Analysis, designing, implementing and Testing a Software Systems to meet specified requirements.

OVERVIEW
The department provides extensive computing resources for research and education. This includes more than 200 high-
performance computers in the range of Linux, Windows, and a good number of laptop systems in 8 laboratories apart
from the special research labs.
In addition to general-purpose file server, the department maintains five specialized servers for database clients;
Linux based workstations. The RYMEC campus network communications include a fiber-optic backbone, offering
10Gbps bandwidth from BSNL (Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited), and Airtel to provide the internet. The department has
wireless internet connectivity via a campus-wide 802.11b network to the laptops. There is a separate department library
with 800 books, manuals, project reports and lecture CD Rom.
